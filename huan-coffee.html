Huan Coffee - Coffee Shop Website

Huan Coffee is a WordPress-based website created to establish a strong digital presence for a coffee shop brand. The website highlights the shop's history, menu offerings, and signature beverages while using a warm and inviting design that reflects the café's atmosphere. The project focuses on clear content organization and user-friendly navigation so visitors can easily explore the brand and its products.

Role: Responsible for customizing the website's search engine optimization (SEO) and developing the layout and content structure of the Home and History pages to improve search visibility and present the coffee shop's brand story in a clear and engaging way.

Features: Coffee shop menu display, brand story section, featured signature drinks, clean navigation structure, and a warm aesthetic design that reflects the café atmosphere.

Technologies Used: WordPress, HTML, CSS, content management system (CMS).
