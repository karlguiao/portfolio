Anibunnylist - Anime Blog Website

Anibunnylist is a WordPress-based anime blog designed to provide anime enthusiasts with organized content such as anime recommendations, reviews, and curated lists. The project focuses on improving content readability through optimized layout design, typography selection, and intuitive navigation, allowing readers to easily explore and enjoy anime articles.

Role: Responsible for customizing the website layout, optimizing typography and page structure, and organizing blog navigation to improve readability and user experience. The role also included configuring WordPress settings, search engine optimization, structuring blog categories, and ensuring clear content presentation across the platform.

Features: Anime blog posts, arranged anime lists, category-based navigation, readable article layout, and structured content organization.

Technologies Used: WordPress, HTML, CSS, content management system (CMS).
